PLANET BUSTERS

Planet Busters is a video game created over the couse of 9 weeks.

Aliens from the future have been abducting humans from across time to construct a museum of human history! Kill Security Robots by the Tens to stop the immensely immoral establishment of extreme evil!! Planet Busters is a game in which you play as one of four playable characters with up to 3 friends in a local co-op rampage of fury and fun.

Artbook

We created Pixel art animations for each character in Planet Busters because of the speed at which we could create high quality, fluid animations. These include running animations and attack animations for each character for each perspective they would be viewed from.
