SPACE ARTWORK

Galileo

(artwork 3)

This artwork is based off of the difficulties faced by the team that developed the Galileo Mission to Jupiter when it was delayed and called into question after the Space Shuttle Challenger Disaster of 1986. It depicts Galileo’s arrival at jupiter in 1995, 9 years after it’s intended launch. Created with Illustrator, Blender, and Photoshop
